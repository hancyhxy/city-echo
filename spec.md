App Design
How we collect data on their experiences/mood/story
Optionally, at the end of a song, a user can post their experiences/mood/story regarding this specific song. We collect the data on all of this to feedback to the shared soundscape so we know the mood of users when listening to a song. Other users also have access to these experiences/stories attached to this music, to create a shared experience between users. So when other users listen to that song, they can relate to each other on their shared experiences. Location is also taken into account for the location based experience. Users can also attach photos of the location they were at, to share their experience so that other users can relate to them.
Features
Location based Experience
When people are at a location and listening to music, the most played song/playlist at that location, becomes assigned to the location. E.g when people are at a park, there is a specific calming song/playlist people are listening to, therefore, that park automatically is assigned that song/playlist. The user has the choice of choosing this shared song/playlist or choosing their own music. These locations are flexible to change, so if more people starting listening to something else then the music at that location changes.
Music Filtering
Location based music also can be filtererd by mood, artists, feelings, styles, language etc. based off of our data collection method. This is user specific and is designed for them to feel comfortable when they are in specfic moods/situations/mentalities/feelings etc.
Integrated music map (explore mode)
The user will have access to a local map (looks like google maps) but the suggested landmarks are not resturants/petrol stations etc. but songs with many users' different experiences, stories, and moods. Almost as if its they are like pop up reccomendations at a location. So when users are looking to explore different music.
Saving Playlist based system (on/off)
Users create playlists at specific locations. These playlists can include their own songs they like, other peoples songs they have liked based on that other persons experience/mood/story. When they enter a new location they can either choose their playlist, or the location based experience feature we have. Users have the option of using this feature when they want or turn it off and listen to their own music based off their mood/feelings etc. But they will still be prompted by our data collection method.
Friend System
When Users are in explore mode or using the location based experience, the people who have posted their expeirences/mood/stories are available to be befriended on the app.
This allows them to communicate, share songs, and share experiences/stories with each other. Sharing concerts, festivles, etc.
